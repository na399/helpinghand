HelpingHand

Being diagnosed with cancer brings great complications into everyday life, and repeatedly asking for help can become yet another heavy burden to overcome. Patients often don't know what to expect from their close relatives or how to express their needs efficiently. HelpingHand aims at providing an easy to use interface that enables sharing a weekly schedule that only inner circles of close friends or relatives have access to. This way, any of these circles members can volunteer to help in the tasks shared by the user. Moreover, an AI system provides insights on the state of the patient and suggests new tasks he might not come up with.
